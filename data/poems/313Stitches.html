#313–Stitches


Responding to the weekend prompt 313 Stitch here https://sammiscribbles.wordpress.com/2023/05/27/weekend-writing-prompt-313-stitch/ for a 10-word poem or a piece of prose.


Stitches
Insubstantial links
Each strengthens another —
The ties that bind.
