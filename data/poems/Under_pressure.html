Under pressure


Written in the form of an Elfchen …


Modern life subjects us to a barrage of persuasion; to do, to be, to think —to conform.


Words
Great deceivers
Duplicitous solicitous hussies
Incessant narrative compels —seduces
Capitulation




--- End of poem ---
