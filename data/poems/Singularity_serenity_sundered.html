Singularity, serenity, sundered!

I remember singularity
We were one, complete
You and I and them



I recall serenity
We were calm, content
You and I and them



I recollect separation
We were rent, sundered
You and I and them



I realise sorrow
We were many, apart
You and I and them



I endure separation
We were split, adrift
You and I and them.



I suffer solitude
We are lost, seeking
You for I for them.



Singularity, serenity, sundered!
We grope still in darkness
You for I for them
