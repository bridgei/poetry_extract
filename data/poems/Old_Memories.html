Old Memories …


It’s been quiet in the park today


It's been quiet in the park today
Little to harsh the grass's sway
In the softest autumn breeze
Little to mask mind's endless tease
Of Septembers long turned to dust



It's been quiet in the park today
Only memories out to play




--- End of poem ---
